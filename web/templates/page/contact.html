Get Involved

undertheInfluence is a work in progress. The site – and its usefulness – will be greatly improved the more people contribute to it. Here’s how you can get involved.

  • Spread the word

We made undertheInfluence to be an illuminating and useful resource for lots of people. If you discover something interesting in the data, tweet it (and give us a nod).


  • Tell us what’s wrong!

The site is in its infancy and there are going to be things that are wrong. Drop us an email and let us a know if you spot anything you think we should know about.


  • Suggestions for how to improve it

If you’ve used the site and can think of ways to make it better, please let us know. It could be a technical solution; a better way of presenting the information; an additional data source you know about. We want your ideas.


  • Offers of help

The site is still in development and any help in improving and extending it is welcome. You could be a coder, a researcher, or a campaigner. Please drop us an email if you have time or skills to offer.
