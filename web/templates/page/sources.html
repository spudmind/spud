Glossary

Lobbyist: a lobbyists attempts to influence the decisions of government. A commercial lobbyist is someone who is paid to do this.

Lobbying agency: a company made up of lobbyists for hire (and normally combined with public relations people as well), who work on behalf of a number of clients, normally corporations.
